Our Mission

Promote AI research, development, and application in Mexico City, creating a collaborative ecosystem between academia, industry, the public sector, and civil society. Through innovative projects with social impact, we seek to position Mexico City as a driver of global change through AI for Good, fostering an environment conducive to the growth of the digital economy.

Our Vision

Position the CDMX Hub as a global benchmark for talent and innovation in artificial intelligence, promoting an AI-driven economy with social impact, equity, sustainability, and equitable access to knowledge.

Young AI Leaders

The Young AI Leaders community was built on AI for Good's belief that the future of AI lies not only in technological advancements but in the people who guide its development. Our community empowers young leaders with the tools, opportunities, and a global stage to drive ethical and responsible AI innovation. By fostering collaboration, creativity, and inclusivity, we are shaping a future where AI becomes a force for good, transforming lives and addressing the world's most pressing challenges.

Innovation is at the heart of the Young AI Leaders Community. With guidance, mentorship, and resources, this youth-led initiative is designed to thrive and create lasting, positive impact. Through initiatives like the Young AI Leaders Community, AI for Good ensures that young people have the skills, platforms, and support to lead the way in building a more inclusive and sustainable digital future.

The Young AI Leaders community is an initiative started by AI for Good. AI for Good is organized by ITU in partnership with over 40 UN Sister Agencies and co-convened with the Government of Switzerland. AI for Good is identifying trustworthy AI applications, building skills and standards, and advancing AI governance for sustainable development.

Mentors YAIL CDMX Hub

The AI ​​for Good Mexico City HUB Mentoring Program is a collaborative initiative that seeks to connect Hub members with leaders in the AI ​​industry across academia, private industry, government, and civil society. Through this program, we promote a culture of knowledge sharing and co-creation with diverse mindsets, accelerating projects that use AI to solve real-world problems. Our mentors are professional social innovators committed to the responsible use of artificial intelligence as a tool for the common good. They share their time, experience, and guidance to support Hub members in developing their ideas, overcoming challenges, and growing as leaders.
